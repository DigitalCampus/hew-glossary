ITNs - ፀረ ጣንጡ ዛንዚራ

Insecticide Treated Nets are mosquito nets impregnated with insecticide that repel, disable or kills mosquitoes coming into contact with it.

ጣንጡ ዘዳኽምን ዝቐትልን ኬሚካል ዝተሓከመ ዛንዚራ ወይ ኣጎበር

IUCD or IUD - ኣብ ዉሽጢ ማሀፀን ዝቕመጥ ናይ ወሊድ መከላኸሊ

Intrauterine contraceptive device (IUCD) or an intrauterine device (IUD). It is an effective, safe, long-acting, cost-effective contraceptive method. It consists of a plastic or metal object that a specially trained health worker or midwife places inside the womb.

ብዝሰልጠነ በዓል ሞያ ዝወሃብ ንነዊሕ ግዜ ዘገልግል ኣብ ዉሽጢ ማሀፀን ዝቕመጥ ናይ ወሊድ መከላኸሊ
