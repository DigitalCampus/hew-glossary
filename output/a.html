Arthropods - ዓንዲ ሑቐ ዘይብሎም ነፍሳት

An invertebrate animal having an external skeleton, a segmented body and jointed appendages/limbs. E.g. insects such as flies and other creatures such as mites that transmit disease.

ዓንዲ ሑቐ ዘይብሎም ነፍሳት

Ascariasis - ወስፋት

Infection of the small intestine with a helminth parasite called Ascaris lumbricoides. Causes discomfort, indigestion, weakness, and sometimes blocking of the gut.

ብሰንኪ ኣስካሪያ ሉምብሪኮይድ ዝተበሃለ ዝመፅእ ሕማም ወስፋት (ፅግዕተኛ መዓንጣ)

Asphyxia - ኣስፊክዝያ

A condition of severe shortage of oxygen to the body that arises from being unable to breathe normally. Asphyxia in the uterus is due to an inadequate supply of oxygen from the mother's blood or a problem in the placenta.

ብሰንኪ ሕፅረት ኦክስጅን ዝመፅእ ሽግር ኮይኑ ክሳብ ውነ ምስሓትን ሞትን ክበፅሕ ይኽእል

Aspiration (Vacuum) - መጢጥካ ምውጋድ

Abortion/Pregnancy Termination using an instrument like a syringe operated by hand that creates a negative pressure to suck out the contents of the uterus.

ዝመጥጥ መሳርሒ ብምጥቃም ካብ ውሽጢ ሰብነትና አየርን ፈሳሲን ምውጋድ

Assess - ምምርማር/ምድህሳስ

The gathering of information about a patient's status (physical, psychological, sociological).

ወሰንቲ ምልክታት ምዝባዕ ጥዕና መሰረት ጌይረካ ምርመራ ጥዕና ምግባር

Assessment - ምርመራ/ዳህሳስ

See Assess

ወሰንቲ ምልክታት ምዝባዕ ጥዕና መሰረት ጌይረካ ምርመራ ጥዕና ናይ ምግባር ኹነት

Attitudes - አመለኻኽታ

Attitudes are relatively constant feelings directed toward something or someone that contains a judgment about whether that something or someone is good or bad.

ብዛዕባ ሓደ ጉዳይ ዘለካ አመለኻኽታ ወይ ስምዒት/ ብፅቡቕ ይኹን ብሕማቕ

Autism - ኦቲዝም (ፀገም ስነ ኣእምሮ)

Autism is a neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by impaired social interaction, verbal and non-verbal communication, and restricted and repetitive behavior. Parents usually notice signs in the first two years of their child's life.

ኣብ እዋን ቁልዕነት ዝርአ ናይ ምዝራብን ናይ ምርድዳእን ፀገም

Autoclave - ኣውቶክሌቭ (መምከኒ)

An autoclave is a device used to sterilize equipment and supplies by subjecting them to high pressure saturated steam at 121°C for around 15–20 minutes depending on the size of the load and the contents.

ደቀቕቲ ተሃዋስያን ምሉእ ንሙሉእ ንምውጋድ ወይ ንምምካን ዝጠቅም መሳርሒ

Autolysis - ዓርሰ ብርሰት

Destruction of a cell through the action of its own enzymes. Also known as 'self-digestion'.

ናይ ዋህዮታት ወይ ቲሹ ዓርሰ ብርሰት ወይ ዓርሰ ጥፍኣት

Autonomy - ነፃነት

The term refers to every individual's right of self-determination, independence and freedom to make their own choices.

ካብ ናይ ካልኦት አካላት ፀቕጢ ነፃ ኮይንካ ምውሳን

Related to the axilla (Armpit)
