Face - ገፅ ሰብ

The surface of the front of the head that extends from the upper ridge of the eye to the nose and chin (lower jaw).

Fats - ጮማ /ስብሒ

Naturally oily substance, insoluble in water, found in animal bodies and certain seeds. E.g. butter and oil.

Feedback - ግብረ መልሲ

The mechanism of assessing what has happened to the receivers after the communication has occurred. A communication is said to have feedback when the receiver of the message gives his or her responses back to the sender of the message.

Fermentation - ጥጅእ

Chemical change caused by the action of an organic substance such as yeast, involving production of heat. E.g. when sugar is converted into alcohol.

In humans, fertilisation is defined as the fusion of the sperm nucleus with the ovum nucleus. For fertilisation to occur, sperm must be deposited in the vagina no earlier than three days before ovulation or within one day after ovulation.

Fever - ትኩሳት ወይ ረስኒ

Fibre - ሓሰር መሰር

A mixture of different carbohydrates which are not digested like other nutrients but pass through the gut nearly unchanged. Foods rich in fibre are 'kocho'; vegetables like cabbage, 'kosta', carrots, cassava; fruits like banana and avocado; peas and beans; whole-grain cereals.

ክሓቁ ዘይኽእሉ ሕዋስ ዝተፈላለዩ ካርቦሃይድሬትስ ኮይኖም ብመዓናጡ ምንም ዓይነት ለውጢ ከይገበሩ ዝሓልፉ ኢዮም:: ከም ቆጮ ካወሎ ቀስጣ ካሮት ካሳቫ ባናና ኣቮካዶ ዓተር ለውዝን ጥረምረን ድማ ቀንዲ ኣብነታት እዮም::

Fluoride - ፈሎራይድ

Fluoride is a substance found in nature that strengthens teeth and helps prevent tooth decay. Most water systems naturally contain some fluoride, but the amounts must be 'just right' to promote oral health. Not getting enough fluoride in the diet or water supply increases the risk of tooth decay.

Fontanel - መንበሰብስቲ

A fontanel or 'soft-spot' is the space created by the joining of two or more sutures. It is covered by thick membranes and the skin on the baby's head, protecting the brain underneath the fontanel from contact with the outside world.

መንበስብስቲ ማለት ኣብ መንጎ ክልተ ወይ ካቡኡ ንላሕሊ መራኸቢ ሽክና ርእሲ ዝተፈጥረ ሉስሉስ ክፍትት ርእሲ እዩ :: ብጠንካራ ቆርቦት ርእሲ ዝተሸፈነ ኮይን ንሓንጎል ድማ ካብ ሓድጋ ይከላኽል

Increasing the food value of certain food by adding vitamins.

ቫይታሚን ብውሳኽ ትሕዝቶ ምግቢ ምብልፃግ

Fracture - ስብራት

A fracture is where there is a break in a bone caused by a force applied to the body. The degree of damage depends on the magnitude of force applied and the strength of the bone.

Freedom - ናፅነት

The condition of being free, independent.

The most commonly used type of freeze indicator is the freeze-tag. This is an irreversible temperature indicator that shows if a product, such as a vaccine, has been exposed to freezing.

The number of times that an event occurs within a specific period.

The rounded top of the uterus, between the junctions with the two fallopian tubes.
