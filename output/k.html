KMC - ኣተሓቃቁፋ ካንጋሮ

Kangaroo Mother Care (KMC), called after the way that kangaroos look after their young, has been shown to be an extremely effective method of caring for preterm and low birth weight babies. It involves holding a newborn in skin-to-skin contact, day and night, prone and upright on the chest.

ካብ ዕድሚኦም ቀዲሞም ዝተወለዱን ክውለዱ ከለዉ ክብደቶም ትሑት ዝኾኑ ህፃናት እኹል ሙቐት ንክረኽቡ ዝግበር ናይ ቆርበት ንቆርቦት ኣተሓቃቅፋ እዩ::

Knowledge - ፍልጠት

The collection and storage of information and experience. Good quality health education relies on the provision of correct, credible, simple and understandable facts and information. Providing knowledge is about helping someone who has a problem, in the case of your job a health problem, so you will have to be aware of the possible health problems, their consequences and ways of preventing them.

ፍልጠትን ተመኩሮን ምእካብን ምጥቃምን የጠቃልል:: ፅሬቱ ዝሓለወ ኣስተምህሮ ጥዕና ንመሃብ ጭቡጥ መረዳእታ መሰረት ዝገበረ ክኸውን ይግባእ:: ፍልጠት ምክፋል ፀገም ዘለዎ ሰብ ንምሕጋዝ ይጠቅም::

Kwashiorkor - ኩዋሽኮር

Severe form of acute malnutrition characterised by swollen feet, hands and face, and peeling sores. It is caused by not eating enough protein.

ብሕበጥ ሰብነት ዝግለፅ ሕፅረት ፕሮቲን ዝሓዘ ምግቢ ምምጋብ ዝመፅእ ሕማም
